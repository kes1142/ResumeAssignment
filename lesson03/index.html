Vintage Magazine Ad Reimagined

This is my recreation of a 1960s black and white ad for Fender Guitars. All of the text remains the same as the original, but the vibrant color scheme and modern font bring the original ad’s same message into this century. The simple layout and clean lines should stand out among the more common dark and dynamic ads featuring musicians rocking out on stage. I also gave the Fender logo a fresh update.
